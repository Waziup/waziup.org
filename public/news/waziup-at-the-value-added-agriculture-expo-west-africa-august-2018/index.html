WAZIUP at the Value Added Agriculture Expo West Africa August 2018

  • On: Mon Jan 1, 0001

This event showcased the latest construction designs for factories and material to develop sustainable factories together with the equipment and technology supporting the manufacturing industry to increase the production of the final product.

The show was hosted in Ghana the central hub for West African Agriculture and was endorsed and supported by the Ministry of Agriculture Ghana.

Frank and Fiifi from iSpace attended and represented the WAZIUP program and presented on Technology in agriculture.
